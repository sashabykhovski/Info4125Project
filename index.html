About

Welcome to your go-to solution for smart cooking ideas to save money on groceries and minimize food waste! Our platform is designed to support college students who are new to cooking and meal planning as they transition into off-campus life. Without the convenience of meal plans, many students find themselves struggling with over-purchasing, food spoilage, and the frustration of unused ingredients. That's where we come in!

Mission Statement

We offer a user-friendly, web-based platform that makes meal planning easy and efficient. By taking your dietary preferences and grocery list items, our platform will curate easy-to-make recipes with only a few additional ingredients you need, reducing food waste and keeping your budget in check. You'll find recipe suggestions that prioritize perishable items for immediate use, helping you make the most of your groceries and avoid unnecessary waste. With built-in features to input your ingredients and accessible recipe options, our platform brings convenience and sustainability to every meal.
